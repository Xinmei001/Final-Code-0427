Our Method

Derived from the many assessments of Mental Health America (MHA), this “Your Covid Cure” quiz screens Boston University students and others alike based on years of research that the organization has accomplished.

Tweaked to better-fit the “student experience” during a pandemic, this assessment stands to “prescribe” individuals with curated “cures” to alleviate their mental health struggles.
